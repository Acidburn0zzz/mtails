Join our XMPP chat room to ask questions about Tails.

To do so, you need an XMPP account. Some emails providers, such as Riseup, provide XMPP accounts as well.

Then configure your preferred instant messaging client, for example Pidgin, which runs on Windows, GNU/Linux, and macOS, to connect to:

  • server: conference.riseup.net
  • room: tails
  • Use TLS/SSL to connect!
