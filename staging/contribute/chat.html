In addition to the tails general chatroom, we have a dedicated chatroom for contributors.

To join it, use your preferred instant messaging client that supports XMPP, e.g. Pidgin, which runs on Windows, GNU/Linux and macOS.

Here are the needed technical details:

  • protocol: XMPP
  • server: conference.riseup.net
  • channel: tails-dev
  • Use TLS/SSL to connect!
