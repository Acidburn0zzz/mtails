Tails is a portable operating system that protects your privacy and avoids censorship.

[[!img avoid.png link="no" alt=""]]

Avoid surveillance, censorship, advertising, and viruses

Tails uses the Tor network to protect your privacy online and avoid censorship. Enjoy the Internet like it should be.

[[!img anywhere.png link="no" alt=""]]

Your secure computer anywhere

To use Tails, shutdown the computer and start on your Tails USB stick. Tails leaves no trace on the computer when shutting down.

[[!img toolbox.png link="no" alt=""]]

Digital security toolbox

Tails includes tools to work on sensitive documents and communicate securely. Everything in Tails is ready-to-use and secure by default.

[[!img gift-heart.png link="no" alt=""]]

Free Software

You can download Tails for free and security researchers can verify our work. Tails is based on Debian GNU/Linux.
