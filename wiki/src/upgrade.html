Choose your manual upgrade scenario

Upgrade from another Tails

If you know someone of trust who already did the upgrade, you can upgrade your Tails by cloning from it.

You need:

  • Your USB stick
  • Another USB stick with Tails up-to-date
  • 10 minutes to upgrade
