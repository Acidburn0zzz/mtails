Tails is a portable operating system that protects your privacy and avoids censorship.

Start any computer from your Tails USB stick instead of starting on Windows or macOS.

[[!img andres/camping.png link="no" alt=""]]

Your secure computer anywhere

Start any computer from your Tails USB stick to turn it into a secure machine, even somebody else's computer.

[[!img andres/bushes-watching.png link="no" alt=""]]

Avoid surveillance and censorship

Tails uses the Tor network to protect your privacy online and avoid censorship.

[[!img andres/city.png link="no" alt=""]]

No advertising, no viruses, no tracking

Enjoy the Internet like it should be.

Digital security toolbox

Tails includes a selection of tools to work on sensitive documents and communicate securely.

Secure by default

Tails and all its tools are configured with security in mind to make them easy to use and prevent mistakes.
